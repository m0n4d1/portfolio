My Functional World

I have been investigating functional programming for a short while and find it to be most intuitive. I am learning Haskell and it is wonderfully permissive of all the things I have been trying to coerce other languages into doing. Treating programs as sets of transformations on data aligns very well with my default mode of thinking. I love the declarative and concise nature of recursive algorithms although unfortunately most imperative languages consult memory when calling a function which limits the effectiveness of recursion in such languages.
